Titel:             Cannon Conquest


Description:       2D tactical puzzler, free with over 100 levels


Full text [US]:

Cannon Conquest is a challenging 2D Puzzle shooter.
Try to assimilate all enemy cannons and defending your own cannons.

<u>Highlights:</u>

<b> * Four different worlds with different elements and in total over 100 levels </b>

<b> * Optional local multiplayer over bluetooth </b>

 * 4 difficulties per level

 * Online highscore and account system

 * Six different tower types and many other gameplay variations

 * And <i>extremely</i> challenging fourth difficulty

 * No ads, all levels are unlockable by skill alone

<u>Permissions:</u>

<b>Internet:</b> For Online Highscore and account synchronization
<b>Bluetooth:</b> For local multiplayer
<b>Location:</b> For whatever reasons bluetooth needs location permissions...
<b>In-App Products:</b> To unlock worlds without enough points
